Our Story

Born from the heart of Cambodia

Srok Souvenirs was built to share the beauty of Khmer craftmanship with the world with one handmade piece at a time. Every product we carry carries a story of our homeland.

What we stand for

Our Mission

Support Local Artisans

Every purchase directly supports Cambodian craftspeople, cooperatives, and family-run workshops across the country.

Preserve Khmer Culture

We work to keep traditional techniques — silk weaving, silversmithing, basketry — alive for future generations.

Share cambodia's story

We believe every souvenir is an ambassador. Through our products, we bring Cambodian heritage to homes around the world.
